JobBait - AI Job Scam Detection Tool
AI-powered system that analyzes job posts and conducts structured scam risk detection to help users identify and avoid fruadulent listings.

The Problem
College students, graduates, and entry-level job seekers are struggling to find honest work due to an increasing number of job scams. 
Many listings seem legitimate but have subtle warning signs such as unrealistic starting pay, unverifiable companies, mismatched emails, or vague job descriptions.
Most job seekers lack a clear, systematic way to confirm whether a job listing is both real and trustworthy.

The Solution
JobBait evaluates job posting susing a structured multi-factor scoring system. 
Instead of relying on pure judgement or standard "safe vs scam" classification, the system analyzes listings across multiple risk dimensions 
and generates a detailed report with a final evaluation score. 
This will improve consistency, transparency, and decision-making when browsing job opportunities.

Key Features
AI-powered analysis of job postings
Multi-factor risk scoring system
Structured evaluation across key scam indicators
Coversational output explaining potiential risks and how to navigate scams
Repeatable and consistent evaluation logic
